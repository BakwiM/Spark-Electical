Meet the Owner

Mohau Kagiso Mokoena is a Qualified Red Seal Electrician (QCTO) with over 5 years of industrial and facilities maintenance experience. He brings strong expertise in fault finding, preventative maintenance, three-phase systems, motors, starters, and plant operations.

Currently serving in Maintenance & Operations Management, he combines hands-on electrical skills with leadership, safety compliance, and cost control to ensure every project is completed professionally and efficiently.

His experience spans industrial and commercial installations, electrical diagnostics, breakdown response, and compliance with OHS safety standards. With a strong commitment to quality workmanship and reliability, he ensures clients receive safe, long-lasting electrical solutions.
