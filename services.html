Our Electrical & Plumbing Work

Certified. Reliable. Professional.

Professional Electrical Installations

We provide safe and precise electrical installations for residential and commercial properties. Every project is completed according to South African electrical regulations and industry standards. From new wiring to system expansions, we ensure all connections are secure, properly insulated, and professionally tested. Our focus is long-term reliability, not quick fixes. Safety, compliance, and quality workmanship are guaranteed on every installation.

DB Board Upgrades

Outdated or overloaded distribution boards can pose serious safety risks. We professionally upgrade DB boards to improve power distribution, circuit protection, and overall system performance. Each upgrade is carefully planned to ensure correct load balancing and proper circuit labeling. All components are installed in compliance with national safety standards. The result is a safer, more efficient electrical system you can rely on

Geyser Installations

We specialize in the safe installation and replacement of electric and solar geysers. Every installation is completed with proper wiring, secure mounting, and compliant plumbing connections. Safety valves, thermostats, and electrical components are thoroughly tested before completion. Our work ensures optimal performance while meeting regulatory requirements. Reliable hot water solutions installed with precision and care.

Wiring & Fault Detection

Accurate wiring inside an electrical panel is critical for safety and performance. We carefully configure and connect each circuit to ensure correct load distribution and secure terminations. Every wire is properly labeled, tightened, and tested to meet national electrical standards. Our attention to detail reduces the risk of faults, overloads, and future failures. Safe, compliant, and professionally executed wiring you can trust

Electrical Inspections & CoC

We conduct thorough electrical inspections to ensure your installation meets South African compliance standards. Each inspection includes detailed testing of wiring, circuits, and safety components. If compliant, we are authorized to issue a Certificate of Compliance (CoC) for property transfers or legal requirements. Our inspections provide peace of mind and verified safety assurance. Professional certification backed by hands-on expertise.
